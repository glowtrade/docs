---
title: Order Types
description: "Available order types for perpetual futures trading."
---

## Market Order

Executes immediately at the best available price. Use when you want instant entry or exit.

- **Pros**: Guaranteed execution
- **Cons**: May experience slippage in low-liquidity markets

## Limit Order

Executes only at your specified price or better. Use when you want price precision.

- **Pros**: No slippage, you control the price
- **Cons**: May not fill if the market doesn't reach your price

## Stop Loss

Automatically closes your position when the price hits a specified level, limiting your downside.

- Set below the current price for long positions
- Set above the current price for short positions

## Take Profit

Automatically closes your position when the price hits your target, locking in gains.

- Set above the current price for long positions
- Set below the current price for short positions

## TP/SL Combo

Set both Take Profit and Stop Loss simultaneously when opening a position for full risk management.

<Warning>
  Stop Loss and Take Profit orders are triggered by the **mark price**, not the last traded price. This prevents manipulation via low-volume trades.
</Warning>
