## Leverage

Leverage allows you to open positions larger than your available margin. Glo Exchange supports up to **50x leverage** on major assets.

| Asset Class | Max Leverage |
|------------|-------------|
| BTC, ETH | 50x |
| Major Crypto (SOL, XRP, etc.) | 20x |
| Stocks | 20x |
| Indices | 20x |
| Commodities | 20x |
| Forex | 20x |

<Warning>
  Higher leverage increases both potential gains and potential losses. A 50x leveraged position can be liquidated by a 2% adverse price move.
</Warning>

## Margin Types

### Cross Margin
Your entire account balance is used as collateral for all open positions. This provides more buffer against liquidation but puts your full balance at risk.

### Isolated Margin
Each position has its own dedicated margin. If liquidated, only the margin allocated to that position is lost. Other positions and your remaining balance are unaffected.

## Liquidation

A position is liquidated when its margin ratio falls below the **maintenance margin requirement**.

### Liquidation Price

Your liquidation price is displayed when opening a position and on your positions panel. It depends on:

- **Entry price**
- **Leverage used**
- **Position size**
- **Maintenance margin rate**

### Avoiding Liquidation

- Use lower leverage (5x–10x recommended for beginners)
- Always set stop-loss orders
- Monitor your positions regularly
- Add margin to positions approaching liquidation
