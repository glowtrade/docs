## Fee Structure

Glo Exchange charges trading fees on each executed trade. Fees are deducted from your USDC margin balance.

| Fee Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| **Taker** (market orders) | 0.035% |
| **Maker** (limit orders) | 0.01% |
| **Builder Fee** | Included in taker fee |

<Info>
  Maker orders that add liquidity to the orderbook receive a lower fee rate. Limit orders that fill immediately (cross the spread) are charged taker fees.
</Info>

## Fee Examples

| Trade Size | Taker Fee | Maker Fee |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| $1,000 | $0.35 | $0.10 |
| $10,000 | $3.50 | $1.00 |
| $100,000 | $35.00 | $10.00 |

## Funding Rate

Funding rates are exchanged between long and short position holders every **8 hours**. The rate varies by asset and market conditions.

- **Positive funding**: Longs pay shorts
- **Negative funding**: Shorts pay longs

Current funding rates are displayed on each asset's trading page.

## No Hidden Fees

- No deposit fees (gas fees apply for bridging)
- No withdrawal fees (Hyperliquid network fees apply)
- No account maintenance fees
- No inactivity fees
