## How Stock Perps Work

- Stock perpetuals are **synthetic** — you don't own the underlying shares
- Prices are tracked via oracle feeds from major equity data providers
- Trading is available **24/7** including weekends and holidays
- During off-market hours, prices may deviate slightly from the last closing price and will converge when markets reopen
