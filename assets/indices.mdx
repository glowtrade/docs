## Overview

Index perpetuals give you exposure to broad market movements without having to trade individual assets. Available indices on Glo Exchange:

## S&P 500 (SP500)

The **S&P 500** tracks the performance of 500 of the largest publicly traded companies in the United States. It's the most widely followed benchmark for U.S. equity markets.

- **Symbol**: SP500
- **Oracle**: Hyperliquid (`xyz:SP500`)
- **Leverage**: Up to 20x
- **Trading Hours**: 24/7

<Info>
  The SP500 perpetual is ideal for traders who want broad U.S. equity exposure without picking individual stocks.
</Info>

## HyperEVM Index (XYZ100)

The **XYZ 100 Index** tracks the performance of the top 100 assets available on the Hyperliquid perpetuals exchange.

- **Symbol**: XYZ100
- **Oracle**: Hyperliquid (`xyz:XYZ100`)
- **Leverage**: Up to 20x
- **Trading Hours**: 24/7
