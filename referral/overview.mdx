## Overview

The Glo Exchange Referral Program rewards you for bringing new traders to the platform. Earn a percentage of the trading fees generated by users who sign up with your referral code.

## Quick Start

1. Go to the **Rewards** page in the app
2. Copy your unique referral code or link
3. Share it with friends and on social media
4. Earn rewards when they trade

<Info>
  Referral rewards are calculated and distributed automatically. Check your earnings on the Rewards page.
</Info>
