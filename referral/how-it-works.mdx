## Referral Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your referral link">
    Navigate to the **Rewards** page and copy your unique referral link (e.g., `glo.exchange?ref=YOUR_CODE`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with others">
    Share your link on social media, Discord, Telegram, or directly with friends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New user signs up">
    When someone signs up using your link, they're permanently linked to your referral code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn rewards">
    You earn a percentage of the builder fees from every trade your referrals make — for as long as they trade on the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking

- **Real-time dashboard**: See your referral count, volume, and earnings on the Rewards page
- **Per-referral breakdown**: View individual referral activity and contribution
- **Google Analytics tracking**: Each referral code is tracked individually for attribution
