## Reward Structure

Referral rewards are paid from the **builder fee** collected on each trade. You earn a percentage of the fees generated by your referred users.

## Payouts

- Rewards are calculated based on trading volume generated by your referrals
- Payouts are processed periodically and credited to your USDC balance
- Check the **Rewards** page in the app for your current earnings and payout history

## Terms

- Referral links never expire — once a user signs up with your code, the referral is permanent
- Self-referrals are not allowed
- Glo Exchange reserves the right to modify the referral program terms
