## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit glo.exchange">
    Go to [glo.exchange](https://glo.exchange) and click **Connect Wallet** in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Privy">
    Sign in using your email, Google account, or connect an existing wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.). Privy creates a secure embedded wallet for you automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Once connected, you'll see the trading dashboard with live market data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Deposit Funds

You need USDC on Hyperliquid to start trading.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bridge from Arbitrum" icon="bridge">
    Bridge USDC from Arbitrum to Hyperliquid directly from the deposit page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Korean CEX On-Ramp" icon="won-sign">
    Deposit from Bithumb or Upbit with automated conversion and bridging.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Minimum deposit is **$10 USDC**. Deposits typically complete within 1-5 minutes depending on network congestion.
</Info>

## Step 3: Place Your First Trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an asset">
    Browse the asset list or use the search bar. Click on any asset to open its trading view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose order type">
    Select **Market** for instant execution or **Limit** to set your price. Adjust leverage using the slider (1x–50x).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set size and direction">
    Enter your position size and click **Long** (buy) or **Short** (sell).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the order summary and confirm. Your position appears in the Positions panel below the chart.
  </Step>
</Steps>
