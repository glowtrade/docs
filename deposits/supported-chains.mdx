## Supported Networks

| Chain | Token | Status |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Arbitrum One | USDC | Supported |
| Ethereum | USDC | Supported (auto-bridged via LI.FI) |

## How Bridging Works

When you deposit USDC from a supported network:

1. You send USDC to your assigned deposit address
2. Glo Exchange detects the incoming transaction
3. Funds are automatically bridged to Hyperliquid via [LI.FI](https://li.fi) cross-chain aggregator
4. USDC is transferred from your spot account to your perp account on Hyperliquid
5. Your trading balance updates and you're ready to trade

<Warning>
  Only send **USDC** to your deposit address. Sending other tokens may result in permanent loss of funds.
</Warning>
