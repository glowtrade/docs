## Funding Your Account

To trade on Glo Exchange, you need **USDC** deposited to your Hyperliquid perp account. There are multiple ways to fund your account:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bridge from Arbitrum" icon="bridge" href="/deposits/supported-chains">
    Bridge USDC from Arbitrum One to Hyperliquid. The standard method for most users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Korean CEX On-Ramp" icon="won-sign" href="/deposits/korean-cex-onramp">
    Deposit directly from Korean exchanges like Bithumb or Upbit with automated bridging.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Deposit Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate deposit">
    Click the **Deposit** button in the app navbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose method">
    Select your preferred deposit method (bridge or CEX on-ramp).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send funds">
    Follow the on-screen instructions to send USDC to your deposit address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic bridging">
    Glo Exchange automatically bridges your funds to Hyperliquid and credits your perp account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Minimum Deposit

The minimum deposit is **$10 USDC**.

## Processing Time

| Method | Typical Time |
|--------|-------------|
| Arbitrum Bridge | 1-5 minutes |
| Korean CEX On-Ramp | 3-10 minutes |

<Info>
  You can track your deposit status in real-time from the deposit page. You'll receive a notification when your funds are ready for trading.
</Info>
