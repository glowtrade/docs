## Overview

Glo Exchange supports direct deposits from Korean centralized exchanges, making it easy for Korean traders to fund their accounts without manual USDC bridging.

## Supported Exchanges

| Exchange | Status |
|----------|--------|
| Bithumb | Supported |
| Upbit | Coming soon |

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Korean CEX deposit">
    Click **Deposit** and choose the Korean CEX On-Ramp option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your deposit address">
    You'll receive a unique deposit address on a supported network (e.g., Arbitrum).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw from your CEX">
    On Bithumb (or Upbit), withdraw USDC to the provided deposit address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic processing">
    Glo Exchange detects the deposit, bridges it to Hyperliquid, and credits your perp account automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The entire process typically takes 3-10 minutes from initiating the CEX withdrawal to having funds available for trading.
</Info>
